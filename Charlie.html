Charles McLean (Charlie) Matthewson
June 18, 1946 - May 13, 1951



CHARLIE MATTHEWSON
4 YEARS OLD
Charles McLean (Charlie) Matthewson was born on June 18, 1946 to Lucy, age 22, and Sonny Matthewson, age 35. He had one older brother, Rod, who had turned 5 exactly a week before.

Charlie had a younger brother, Ralph Wayne, who was born on May 7, 1948, when Charlie was just under 2 years old, but on August 29, 1948, at just under 4 months old, baby Ralph died in his crib at their house, at 8938 - 80th Avenue, Edmonton.

Charlie’s younger sister, Joyce, was born on October 17, 1950, when Charlie was 4 years old.

Sometime between 1949 and 1951, the Matthewsons moved to 11842 - 126th Street, Edmonton.

Charlie was tragically run over and killed on Mother’s Day in 1951. He is buried in the Edmonton Cemetery, Section M, Block 6, Plot 8, in an unmarked grave.
